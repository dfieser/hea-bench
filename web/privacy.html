Privacy Policy

Thermodynamic Descriptor Calculator — last updated June 1, 2026

Short version: This calculator runs entirely in your browser. No personal data is collected. Inputs and saved results stay on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, and there is no analytics or tracking of any kind.

1. No personal data collected

This site does not ask for, collect, or store any personal information such as your name, email address, IP address, or account credentials. There is no registration or login.

2. All calculations run locally

Every calculation is performed entirely within your browser. The element compositions and mole fractions you enter are never transmitted to any server.

3. Browser storage (localStorage)

To preserve your work between sessions, the calculator stores the following data in your browser’s localStorage:

  • Your last entered composition (form state)
  • Calculation results you explicitly saved
  • Any custom element data you added

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser’s site data for this page (browser Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear browsing data, or equivalent).

4. No third-party services

All resources (MathJax for equation rendering, fonts, styles) are loaded from files hosted on this server. No external services, CDNs, analytics, advertising networks, or social media trackers are used.

5. Cookies

This site does not set any cookies. No consent banner is shown because no cookies are placed.

6. Changes to this policy

If the data practices described here change materially, this page will be updated with a revised “last updated” date.

7. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the site owner via dfieser.com.
